ABOUT

Bye Bye to Keyboard and mouse Welcome to new web voice power where you can play audio/video and play a game too just by speaking to your system. So why waiting start giving commands to your system.

Available commands

  • Play - Play the current song
  • Stop - Stop the current song
  • Pause - Pause the current song
  • Next - Play the next song
  • Previous - Plays the previous song
  • Play [song's name] - Plays a song with a particular name, if found

  • Play - Plays the video
  • Stop - Stops the video
  • Pause - Pauses the video
